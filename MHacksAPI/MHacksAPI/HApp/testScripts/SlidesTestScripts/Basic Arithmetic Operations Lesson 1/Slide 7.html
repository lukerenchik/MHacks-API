Braille Symbols for Division

The braille symbol for division is represented by the dots 1, 4, and 5. It is used to indicate that one number should be divided by another.

For example, the braille representation of the number 8 divided by the number 2 is:

    **   **
     *   *
    *     *

This can be read as "8 divided by 2 equals 4".
