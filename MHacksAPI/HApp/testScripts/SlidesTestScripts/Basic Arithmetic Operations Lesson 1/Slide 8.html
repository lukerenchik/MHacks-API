Review and Practice

Review

In this lesson, we have learned about the four basic arithmetic operations and their corresponding braille symbols:

  • Plus sign (+): dots 3-4
  • Minus sign (-): dots 1-4
  • Multiplication sign (x): dots 3-4-5
  • Division sign (/): dots 1-4-5

Practice

Try solving the following problems using the braille symbols:

  2. 5 minus 3 equals ________
  3. 3 multiplied by 4 equals ________
