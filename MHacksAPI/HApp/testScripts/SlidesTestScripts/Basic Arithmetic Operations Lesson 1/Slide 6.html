Braille Symbols for Multiplication

The braille symbol for multiplication is represented by the dots 3, 4, and 5. It is used to indicate that two numbers should be multiplied together.

For example, the braille representation of the number 3 multiplied by the number 4 is:

     *   *
    **   **
    *     *

This can be read as "3 multiplied by 4 equals 12".
